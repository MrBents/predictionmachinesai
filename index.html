Our Services

Our goal at Prediction Machines is to provide machine vision based solutions for the manufacturing industry. By leveraging the latest techniques in computer vision and machine learning, we are able to perform a variety of visual inspection tasks such as quality control for anomaly detection and classification, as well as optical character recognition for locating and interpreting all kinds of serial codes from labels and packaging.

Quality Inspection

Quality inspection is utilized to check for defects and irregularities that might be found in manufactured products.

Hardware Development and Programming

Hardware programming and set up. Embedded device programming for manufacturing purposes as well as PLC and SCADA programming.

Optical Character Recognition

Optical character recognition is extensively used for scanning and identifying labels, barcodes, and texts, especially in the packaging sector.

Icon

Gauging

Gauging systems are used to determine the distance between two or more points with the goal of determining whether the dimensions of a part or product meets specifications.

Icon

Counting and Identification

Counting and identification systems are used to count and identify the number of objects present in a picture. These kinds of systems are used in the pharmaceutical industry.

Statistical Analysis

All data gathered by the machine vision software is later used to perform statistical analysis.
